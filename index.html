Baked Ziti

The best Baked Ziti around town, guaranteed to make your mouth explode within the first 3 BITES! Heres what you are going to need to get started.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lbs ziti pasta
  • 2 chopped onions
  • 26 oz 93/7 lean ground beef
  • 2 cans of three cheese pasta sauce
  • 2 bags of mozzarella cheese
  • 2 bags of American cheese
  • 1 baking pan

Now here's how you prepare this Delicious meal!

Directions

  1. Cook the ground beef until it is brown
  2. Boil the pasta until it is soft
  3. Pour the pasta sauce into the skillet with the beef and heat the pasta up
  4. Layer some of the ground beef at the bottom of the baking pan
  5. Then layer some of the pasta on top of the ground beef
  6. Then layer some of the cheese on top of the pasta
  7. Repeat this process until the entire baking pan is filled up
  8. Put in the oven at 400 0 degrees Fahrenheit and bake until the cheese is melted
