Honey Smoked Salmon

The best Honey Smoked Salmon that you have ever tasted in your life is now here. A recipe that you can either use for your family or fancy date, it is sure to be something special

Here's what you're going to need to get started

Ingredients:

  • 16oz bottle of raw honey
  • 12oz Honey Smoked Salmon
  • Garlic
  • Baking Pan
  • micro-greens
  • 1 lemon

Directions

  1. Pre heat the oven to 3500F
  2. Season the Salmon with salt, pepper and garlic
  3. Chop a half an onion and add it to the baking pan
  4. Put in oven for about 15-20min
  5. cook micro-greens on top of skillet
  6. Take out and enjoy!
