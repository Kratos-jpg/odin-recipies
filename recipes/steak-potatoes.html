Steak and Potatoes Recipe

This is by far my FAVORITE recipe of any food that I've made. Simple quick and effective, that will sure be in your weekly meal plans for futures to come.

Here's what you're going to need to get started

Ingredients:

  • 8oz cut of Filet Mignon
  • 5lb pag of red potatoes
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • onion
  • Your choice of cheese

Now it's time to put it all together!

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 4000F
  2. Boil the potatoes until it gets soft
  3. Put oil in a skillet and heat it up
  4. Put the steak in with the fat cap first ( the side with the most fat)
  5. Cook the fat cap for about 30 seconds and flip it over to do the same to the other side
  6. Then cook the steak on each side for a minute and put it in the oven for 12 minutes
  7. Mash the potatoes, add salt, pepper, cheese, and milk to your liking for the right consistency
  8. Take out the steak and let it rest for a minimum of 3 minutes
  9. Plate the food and enjoy!
